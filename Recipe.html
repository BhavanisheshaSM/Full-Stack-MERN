Anjal Tawa Fry

Ingredients :

  • Anjal Fish (King Fish)
  • Chilly Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Curry Leaves
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Corn Flour
  • Tamarind Pulp
  • Coconut Oil

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Take all your ingredients for marinating fish. You need chilli powder, cumin, coriander, garam masala powder, curry leaves, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, salt, corn flour, tamarind pulp and cocunut oil.
  2. Step 2: Mix all the ingredients given for marination together. I like to use a bowl for mixing the marination. Add in tamarind pulp. Sometimes I add vinegar to the marination. It adds a tangy taste to the marination which is essential for fried fish.
  3. Step 3: Mix the marination together with your hands. Make sure the marination doesn't turn too runny. You want it to be thick.
  4. Step 4: Now you can see the marination looks dry. You may add a splash of water in this and mix. Since this doesn't have fish added yet, you can taste and check for seasoning. Now the marination masala is ready. It has to be thick like paste.
  5. Step 5: Take your fish pieces. Since this is a whole fish, make deep slashes on the fish. The marination will penetrate through these gashes. You can use any fish like king fish steaks, seer fish or even anchovies.
  6. Step 6: Apply the marination all over the fish. Apply evenly into the slashes. Let the fish marinate for minimum 30 minutes to 1 hour
  7. Step 7: I prefer to fry the fish in coconut oil. Take a shallow frying pan, like cast iron or nonstick. Place fish in hot coconut oil and fry on high heat for 4 to 5 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Now flip the fish carefully, reduce the flame and cook on other side for 3 to 4minutes. Remove the fish carefully to serving plate. Garnish with coriander leaves, lemon wedges and onion rings.
